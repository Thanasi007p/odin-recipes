Pizza

Description

This recipe is my favorite pizza recipe in the entire world. There is no recipe better than this one.

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Tomato Sauce

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 400 fahrenheit
  2. Roll dough
  3. Form pizza
  4. Put into the oven for 20 minutes
  5. Take out of oven and let it cool down, then enjoy
